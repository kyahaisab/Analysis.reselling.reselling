Nike brought back another popular Nike Dunk from the early 2000s with the release of the Nike Dunk Low Brazil (2020), now available on StockX. The original Nike Dunk Low Brazil debuted in 2001 in both high and low cut variations.

This Dunk Low Brazil features a yellow leather upper with green overlays and white detailing. A white midsole, green outsole, and yellow Nike branding on the heel complete the design. These sneakers released in May of 2020 and retailed for $100 USD.
